SPACE IBIZA NY

Ibiza’s most legendary nightlife and entertainment brand, Space Ibiza, debuted its first North American location in New York with the opening of Space Ibiza New York on Friday, September 12, 2014. Space Ibiza New York's incarnation brings the unrivaled energy and entertainment of the world’s number one nightclub to Manhattan’s burgeoning Hell’s Kitchen neighborhood. Space Ibiza New York combines the warm intimacy of a private Ibiza club with the stylish, sexy glamour of Manhattan.

Space Ibiza has established itself as a worldwide reference for dance music culture over the past 25 years. It is hallowed ground for both world-renowned and emerging artists in the electric dance and contemporary musical culture. This unmatched credibility and heritage will be leveraged to bring one-of-a-kind performances from the world’s most popular deejays as well as up-and-coming artists to the venue. Securing both resident deejays and special performances, Space Ibiza New York is the hub for patrons looking to party amongst EDM elite, as well as New York’s coveted fashion and art crowds. Replicating it’s counterpart in Ibiza, Space Ibiza New York gives its guests the ultimate nightlife experience, showing patrons a party from the evening hours until the early morning.
